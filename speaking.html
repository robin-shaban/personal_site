02Topics

Antitrust in a Multipolar Digital Economy

Competition policy when the largest digital firms are jurisdictionally fragmented, regulatory approaches diverge across blocs, and developing economies negotiate market access on different terms.

From Competition Act to Digital Compact: Lessons from Canada's Reform Decade

A practitioner account of the 2022 to 2024 Canadian reforms, repositioned as a case study for jurisdictions designing modern competition regimes.

Market structure as a tool for inclusive economic development, drawing on competition economics and the empirical record from multiple jurisdictions.
