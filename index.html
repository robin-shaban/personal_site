•About

Robin Shaban is a Canadian economist and public policy expert whose work concentrates on competition and market structure in digital economies and inclusive growth contexts. Their practice spans commissioned research, public-interest advisory, speaking, and board service.

Commercial competition and regulatory consulting is delivered through 2R Strategy, where Robin is a Partner. Read full bio.

Robin contributed to the 2022 to 2024 Canadian Competition Act reforms through parliamentary testimony and the study Study of Competition Issues in Data-Driven Markets in Canada, commissioned by ISED Canada. They serve as Senior Advisor at the International Identification Code Industry Alliance (ICA) in Singapore, are co-founder and Chair of the Canadian Anti-Monopoly Project, and a Fellow at the Public Policy Forum.
